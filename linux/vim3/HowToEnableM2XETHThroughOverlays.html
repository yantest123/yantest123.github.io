How to enable M2X ETH through overlays

We have already explained in the documentation how to useDevice Tree Overlays.

Enable M2X ETH Under Ubuntu

Update System:

Change The Configuration Of Device Tree Overlays

1、open/boot/env.txtfilm:

uart3 pwm_f i2c3 os08a10are enabled by default.

2、addm2x-ethtooverlaysnode to make M2X ETH enable

3、save the film/boot/env.txt and reboot the board to make it available.
